Where to Stay

There are several places to stay while visiting Assateague Island. Ocean City, Maryland is 20 minutes away from Assateague Island, where visitors can see the beautiful coastal wildlife up close. Chincoteague Island, Virginia is minutes away from Assateague's beach, where visitors can see the wild horses roam the sandy beaches.
