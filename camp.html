Camping

Assateague State Park

This state park offers 342 campsites with a fire ring and picnic table. Some sites have electric hook-ups for RVs and are pet-friendly.

6915 Stephen Decatur Hwy
Berlin, MD 21811

(410) 641-2918

Pocomoke River State Park

This state park offers 175 campsites that come with a picnic table and fire ring. The public washhouses have hot water showers, toilets, and a laundry tub. Some campites have electric hook-ups and dumping stations.

3461 worcester Hwy
Snow Hill, MD 21863

(410) 632-2566

Castaways RV Resort LLC

This 50 acre waterfront propery is located across the bay from Assateague Island National Park. The resort offers 393 campsites that are fully equiped with Wi-Fi, cable TV hook-ups, water, and electric. There are 4 types of campsite options and are pet-friendly.

12612 Eagles Nest Rd
Berlin, MD 21811

(410) 213-2267

Assateague Island National Shore

This protected area offers 5 main campsite types for tents, trailers, and even horse camping. All campsite facilities include access to chemical toilets, cold water showers, and drinking water.

7206 National Seashore Ln
Berlin, MD 21811

(443) 614-3547
